Help & Info - Focus Assistant

User Guide

Focus Assistant helps you concentrate by blocking distracting websites and managing your time effectively with the Pomodoro Timer.

  • Focus Mode: Toggle on/off blocking of websites in your list.
  • Pomodoro Timer: Set up focused work sessions interspersed with breaks.
  • Block List: Add or remove websites you want to block in the Settings page.
  • Schedules: Automatically activate Focus Mode at specific times.
  • Strict Mode: When enabled, you cannot turn off Focus Mode or stop a running Pomodoro.
